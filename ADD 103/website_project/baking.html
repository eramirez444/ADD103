How It Started

My love for baking began when I was a kid watching the Food Network channel. Seeing chefs prepare amazing dishes made me want to try it myself. My very first recipe was red velvet cupcakes. They didn’t turn out the way I wanted, but instead of quitting, I kept practicing until I improved.

Family Influence

Both of my parents shaped my love for food. My dad, a food lover, showed me dishes from his home in Mexico, while my mom, who grew up in the northwest suburbs of Chicago, introduced me to her own home-style recipes. This gave me a balanced view of flavors and techniques.

Why I Bake

Baking became more than just a hobby. It’s my way of relaxing and creating something that makes people happy. Every recipe I try is a chance to learn and share something special.
