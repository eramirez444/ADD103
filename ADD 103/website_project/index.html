Where I Come From

I was born in Libertyville, Illinois, but my family has lived in McHenry County my entire life. On my father’s side, my family is from Ixtapan de la Sal, Mexico. My dad became a U.S. citizen in 2010, which has always inspired me to stay motivated and pursue my goals.

Who I Am

I’m an introverted but creative student who enjoys horror, writing, and baking. I’m currently studying both software development and graphic design at MCC. My sister Jackie—our family’s “gothic sister”—introduced me to horror, and it became a big part of my identity.

Looking Ahead

My dream is to combine technology, design, and storytelling into a career. Whether that means working on creative media, design projects, or storytelling, I want to keep learning and creating things that connect with people.
