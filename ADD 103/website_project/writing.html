My Love for Stories

I’ve always loved reading. Getting lost in books inspired me to create my own stories. Over time, I found myself drawn to writing short horror stories. The suspense and imagination involved make it incredibly fun.

Influences

Horror has been a constant in my life. My sister Jackie, the “gothic sister” in our family, introduced me to the genre. My aunt Michelle let me rent my first horror video back when rental stores were still around. Blockbuster was my sanctuary as a child, and those experiences left a lasting impact on me.

How I Write

When I write, I focus on building atmosphere and suspense. I like short, impactful stories with endings that stay in your mind. Writing is more than a pastime—it’s a way to express creativity, explore emotions, and share ideas that matter to me.
