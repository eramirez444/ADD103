About Me 🍑

🌼 A Love for Plants

I grew up surrounded by flowers and fruit bushes—marigolds, lily-of-the-valley, honeysuckle, raspberries, and blueberries. Gardening taught me patience and process, which translate nicely into design and development. When things grow, it’s because you prepared the soil, tended the basics, and stayed consistent—just like learning code.

🗺️ Illinois Roots & Travel Goals

I was born in Libertyville and have lived in the McHenry County area most of my life. I’d love to travel more and collect reference photos, textures, and color palettes from different places—fuel for future projects and designs.

💻 What I’m Studying

I’m in the Software Application Design & Development program at McHenry County College. I’m strengthening my foundation in semantic HTML and modern CSS, focusing on structure, accessibility, and clear, readable code. My goal is to apply these fundamentals to bigger projects over time.

Quick Facts 🍎

  • Program: Software Application Design & Development (MCC)
  • Focus: HTML/CSS, semantics, accessibility
  • Interests: baking, hiking, poetry, horror writing
  • Palette: neon pastels & citrus accents
